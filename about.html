Our Story

Karibu — Welcome to Our Family

Lund Big5 Tours Limited was founded by a team of born-and-raised East Africans with one purpose: to share the extraordinary beauty of Kenya and Tanzania with the world — authentically, safely, and with genuine heart.

We grew up beside these national parks. We learned the Serengeti's rhythms, watched the Great Migration thunder across the Mara River, and summited Kilimanjaro before we ever imagined turning that passion into a career. When travellers started asking us to guide them, we realised this was more than a business — it was a calling.

Today, Lund Big5 Tours Limited operates from Toronto, Canada — with our field operations rooted in Nairobi, Arusha, and the shores of Zanzibar. Every itinerary is built by our own locally-based guides who know exactly where the lions den at sunset and which crater rim rewards the clearest view.

We've been trusted by over 1,200 families from across the globe, and every five-star review we receive reminds us why we started.

What We Stand For

Our Mission, Vision & Promise

Three principles guide every safari we design and every guest we welcome into East Africa.

Our Mission

To deliver authentic, tailor-made East African safari experiences that connect every traveller to the true soul of Kenya and Tanzania — safely, honestly, and with warmth that lasts long after the journey ends.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted safari partner for families, couples, and adventurers worldwide — known for our transparency, our people, and our commitment to giving every guest the trip of a lifetime.

Travel That Gives Back

We Believe in Responsible Safari

Every journey we craft is designed to leave East Africa — its wildlife, its communities, and its landscapes — better than we found it.

Wildlife Conservation

A portion of every booking supports anti-poaching initiatives and habitat preservation across the parks we visit.

Community First

We work exclusively with locally owned lodges, Maasai-run campsites, and community conservancies to ensure your money flows back to local families.

Low-Impact Travel

We keep group sizes small, follow park guidelines rigorously, and use fuel-efficient vehicles to minimise our environmental footprint on every trip.
