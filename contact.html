Before You Reach Out

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from travellers just like you.

We aim to respond to every email and form inquiry within 24 hours. On WhatsApp we typically respond within minutes — feel free to send us a message anytime and we'll be right with you.
Absolutely — 100% free, no credit card, no obligation. We never charge booking or consultation fees. Share your dates, interests and budget and we'll build a detailed, personalised itinerary just for you.
Yes — we design safaris for solo adventurers, honeymoon couples, families with young children, and corporate groups of 20+. All itineraries are private and built around your specific group, not shared with strangers.
We accept bank transfers, major credit/debit cards, and PayPal. A 30% deposit secures your booking, with the balance due 60 days before departure. We'll walk you through the secure payment steps once you're happy with your itinerary.
We offer flexible cancellation with a full deposit refund for cancellations made more than 90 days before departure. Within 90 days, partial refunds apply based on supplier terms. We strongly recommend travel insurance for all bookings.
Most nationalities require a visa for both Kenya and Tanzania. Kenya offers an e-Visa online (approx. USD $50), and Tanzania's e-Visa is also available online (approx. USD $50). We'll provide full visa guidance for your nationality as part of your booking process.
Kenya and Tanzania are among Africa's most established and well-visited safari destinations. Our local guides know every route intimately, all our vehicles are well-maintained and licensed, and we provide 24/7 on-trip support. Over 1,200 guests have travelled with us safely and happily.
Absolutely — a safari + Zanzibar beach extension is one of our most popular combinations. After days on the savanna, there's nothing better than the white sands and turquoise waters of Zanzibar. We'll arrange seamless transfers and beachfront accommodation to complete your perfect trip.
