Tanzania Destinations

7 extraordinary parks, peaks and shores — all guided by our Tanzania-based team

National Park 🇹🇿
Tanzania

Serengeti National Park

The world's greatest wildlife spectacle — endless golden plains, the annual Great Migration of 1.5 million wildebeest, and some of Africa's densest predator populations.

Great Migration Lion Prides Leopard Cheetah Big Five
Best: Jun – Oct View Details
World Heritage 🇹🇿
Tanzania

Ngorongoro Crater

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and natural wonder — the world's largest intact volcanic caldera shelters 25,000 large animals in a self-contained ecosystem.

Black Rhino Flamingos Lions Hippos Big Five
Best: Year-round View Details
National Park 🇹🇿
Tanzania

Tarangire National Park

Famous for its giant ancient baobab trees and some of the largest elephant herds in Africa. Quieter and more intimate than the Serengeti, yet equally spectacular.

Elephant Herds Baobab Trees Wildebeest Tree-climbing Lions
Best: Jun – Oct View Details
National Park 🇹🇿
Tanzania

Lake Manyara

A shallow alkaline lake that turns pink with thousands of flamingos, set beneath a dramatic Great Rift Valley escarpment. Home to unique tree-climbing lions and hippo pools.

Flamingos Tree-climbing Lions Hippo Pools Birding
Best: Jul – Mar View Details
National Park 🇹🇿
Tanzania

Arusha National Park

A compact yet diverse park at the foot of Mount Meru, offering canoeing on Momela Lakes, giraffe spotting and walking safaris just 45 minutes from Arusha city.

Mount Meru Colobus Monkeys Giraffe Canoe Safaris
Best: Year-round View Details
Trekking 🇹🇿
Tanzania

Mount Kilimanjaro

Africa's highest peak at 5,895m — a bucket-list climb for trekkers of all abilities. Choose from Marangu, Machame, Lemosho or Rongai routes with our certified mountain guides.

Uhuru Peak 5,895m Marangu Route Machame Route 5–9 Day Treks
Best: Jan–Mar · Jun–Oct View Details
Beach & Culture 🇹🇿
Tanzania

Zanzibar Island

The perfect safari extension — turquoise Indian Ocean waters, powdery white beaches, UNESCO-listed Stone Town, spice plantation tours, and world-class snorkelling at Mnemba Atoll.

White Sand Beaches Stone Town Mnemba Snorkelling Spice Tours
Best: Jun – Oct View Details

Kenya Destinations

6 iconic parks and reserves — led by our Kenya-born, Nairobi-based guide team

Must Visit 🇰🇪
Kenya

Masai Mara

Kenya's crown jewel and one of the most celebrated wildlife reserves on earth — home to the Big Five year-round and the Great Migration river crossings between July and October.

Big Five Great Migration Hot Air Balloon Maasai Culture
Best: Jul – Oct View Details
National Park 🇰🇪
Kenya

Amboseli National Park

A photographer's paradise — stunning views of Kilimanjaro's snow-capped peak rising above vast herds of free-roaming elephants, flamingo-dotted swamps and vast open plains.

Elephant Herds Kilimanjaro Views Flamingos Photography
Best: Jun – Oct View Details
National Park 🇰🇪
Kenya

Tsavo National Park

Kenya's largest national park — a vast, rugged landscape of lava flows, baobab forests and savanna. Home to the famous red elephants, maneless Tsavo lions, and over 60 different mammal species.

Red Elephants Tsavo Lions Hippo Pools Off the Beaten Track
Best: Jun – Oct View Details
National Park 🇰🇪
Kenya

Lake Nakuru

Situated in the Great Rift Valley, Lake Nakuru is a haven for birdlife — most famously millions of flamingos that turn the lake shore pink. Also an important sanctuary for both black and white rhinos.

Flamingos Black & White Rhino Leopard Birdwatching
Best: Year-round View Details
Reserve 🇰🇪
Kenya

Samburu National Reserve

A remote northern gem home to the Samburu Special Five — Grevy's zebra, reticulated giraffe, Beisa oryx, Somali ostrich and gerenuk — species found nowhere else on any Kenyan safari circuit.

Grevy's Zebra Reticulated Giraffe Gerenuk Samburu Culture
Best: Jun – Sep View Details
Trekking 🇰🇪
Kenya

Mount Kenya

Africa's second highest peak at 5,199m — a dramatic mountain of glaciers, alpine lakes and afromontane forest. The Sirimon and Chogoria routes offer rewarding ascents for experienced trekkers.

Batian Peak 5,199m Sirimon Route Alpine Lakes Wildlife Trekking
Best: Jan–Feb · Jul–Sep View Details
