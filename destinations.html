    Full Programme

    Day-by-Day Itinerary

    Your East African adventure begins the moment you land. Our guide will meet you at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (NBO) — you'll be holding a sign with your name, so you won't miss us. We transfer you to your hotel in Nairobi for check-in and freshening up.

    In the evening, we gather for a pre-safari briefing over a welcome dinner, where we'll walk you through the itinerary, answer all questions, and share what to expect on the Mara. You'll meet your lead guide and receive your safari kit.

    Airport meet & greet transfer to Nairobi hotel
    Pre-safari orientation and briefing session
    Welcome dinner at a local restaurant
    Overnight: Nairobi hotel (B&B)
    Dinner

    After an early breakfast, we load up the 4WD safari vehicle and begin the scenic 5–6 hour drive to the Masai Mara National Reserve, Kenya's most celebrated wildlife destination. En route, we travel through the Great Rift Valley with breathtaking views and stop at a scenic viewpoint for photos and tea.

    We arrive at camp in time for a hot lunch and check-in to your luxury tented accommodation. After a short rest, we head straight into the reserve for your first afternoon game drive — a perfect introduction to the Mara's abundance of wildlife.

    Early breakfast & 4WD departure from Nairobi
    Great Rift Valley scenic viewpoint stop
    Arrival & check-in at luxury tented camp
    Afternoon game drive (3–4 hours) in the reserve
    Sundowner drinks on the Mara plains
    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

    Rise before dawn for a sunrise game drive — the best time to spot predators returning from the night hunt. Lions, cheetahs and hyenas are most active in the early morning hours, and the golden light makes for spectacular photography.

    We break for a bush picnic lunch on the savanna — a uniquely Masai Mara experience. The afternoon brings another multi-hour game drive with a focus on the Mara River crossing zones, where wildebeest brave crocodile-filled waters during the Great Migration (Jul–Oct).

    5:30 AM pre-dawn wake-up & sunrise game drive
    Big Five tracking with your expert naturalist guide
    Bush picnic lunch in the reserve
    Afternoon Mara River crossing drive (migration season)
    Optional: Hot air balloon safari at dawn (add-on, $450)
    Night at luxury tented camp
    Breakfast Bush Picnic Dinner

    Begin with an early morning game drive before returning to camp for a relaxed breakfast. The highlight of today is a Maasai village visit — a genuine cultural exchange where you're welcomed into a traditional Maasai boma (village), learn about their customs, witness traditional dances, and hear the fascinating story of the Maasai people who have coexisted with the Mara's wildlife for centuries.

    The afternoon is yours to relax at camp before a final sundowner game drive — cold Tusker beer in hand, watching the sun melt into the horizon over the golden plains of the Mara.

    Morning game drive — birds and predator tracking
    Maasai village cultural visit & traditional dance
    Optional guided bush walk with a Maasai warrior
    Camp relaxation: pool, reading, photography
    Sundowner game drive & drinks on the Mara
    Camp farewell dinner under the stars
    Breakfast Lunch Farewell Dinner

    One last sunrise on the Mara. We make the most of the morning with a final early game drive, then return to camp for a hot breakfast and check-out. Safari bags are packed and we begin the scenic drive back to Nairobi.

    Depending on your onward flight, we can arrange a city tour of Nairobi or take you directly to the airport. Our guide will see you through to your departure gate — and you'll leave with memories of East Africa that will last a lifetime. Kwa heri! (Farewell!)

    Final sunrise game drive (depart 6:00 AM)
    Return to camp for breakfast & check-out
    Scenic road transfer back to Nairobi (5–6 hrs)
    Optional: Nairobi city tour or Giraffe Centre visit
    Airport drop-off at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport
    Breakfast Road Lunch
