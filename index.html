Welcome to Big 5 Africa Safari

Your Trusted Home for Unforgettable African Safaris

Karibu — welcome! Big 5 Africa Safari is a trusted, locally-rooted safari company helping travelers from around the world experience the very best of East Africa. From the sweeping plains of the Serengeti and the Masai Mara to the snow-capped summit of Kilimanjaro and the turquoise shores of Zanzibar, we turn once-in-a-lifetime dreams into beautifully crafted journeys.

Every trip is designed by our own team of born-and-raised guides who know exactly where the wildlife gathers and how to make each day effortless. With transparent pricing, careful attention to detail, and a genuine love for our home, we've earned the trust of hundreds of happy guests — and we'd be honoured to welcome you next.
