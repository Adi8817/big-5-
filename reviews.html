Standing at the Mara River: A Moment I'll Never Forget

I've travelled to over 60 countries. Nothing — not the temples of Kyoto, not the Norwegian fjords, not the canals of Venice — has ever moved me the way watching 40,000 wildebeest surge into the crocodile-infested Mara River did. Our guide had studied the herd movements all morning. He positioned us at a small bend, away from the tourist convoy, just as the lead animals committed. The sound alone was seismic. I stood there, camera forgotten, just breathing it in.

Robert Tanaka · 🇨🇦 Canada Great Migration Safari · August 2025

Our Honeymoon: Serengeti, Crater & Zanzibar

We asked the team to design a honeymoon that mixed adventure with luxury. What they delivered exceeded every expectation. Private sundowners, a champagne surprise in the crater, and three perfect days on Zanzibar's North Coast.

Priya & Anil · 🇺🇸 USA February 2026

Solo in the Crater: Travelling Alone, Never Lonely

As a solo female traveller, I was nervous. Within an hour on my first game drive I felt completely safe and genuinely looked after. The Ngorongoro Crater floor is one of the most spectacular places on the planet — I had it almost to myself at dawn.

Greta Hoffmann · 🇦🇺 Australia October 2025
