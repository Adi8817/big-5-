Why Travel With Big 5 Africa Safari?

Six things that set every Big 5 Africa Safari tour apart from every other option in East Africa.

Experienced Safari Guides

Born-and-raised local guides who grew up in the parks and reserves they escort you through. The level of ecological knowledge, language fluency and wildlife intuition is unmatched by any foreign-operated company.

Fully Custom Itineraries

No two Big 5 Africa Safari itineraries are ever identical. Every tour is built from scratch around your specific travel dates, interests, budget and group composition. You choose — we execute perfectly.

Premium Accommodation

We partner only with lodges, tented camps and beach resorts that meet our own quality standard — well-located, well-managed and genuinely immersed in the natural environment rather than isolated from it.

24/7 On-Trip Support

Our base team monitors your safari in real time from the moment you land to the moment you depart. Any issue, any question, any change of plan — we are one WhatsApp message away, any hour of the day or night.

Transparent Pricing

Every quote is fully itemised — park fees, transfers, accommodation, guide, meals and all activities. No hidden charges on arrival, no upselling pressure on the ground. What you see in writing is exactly what you pay.

Trusted Local Experts

Six years of continuous five-star reviews across Google, TripAdvisor and SafariBookings. Over 1,200 guests served across 30+ countries. Our reputation is built entirely on delivering exactly what we promise.
