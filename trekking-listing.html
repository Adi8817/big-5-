🇹🇿 Arusha National Park, Tanzania

Mount Meru — Africa's Best-Kept Trekking Secret

Towering above Arusha at 4,566m, Mount Meru is Tanzania's second highest peak — and one of the most underrated treks on the continent.

The Momella Route winds through wildlife-rich rainforest where you'll encounter giraffe, buffalo and colobus monkeys before emerging onto the dramatic crater rim. The views of Kilimanjaro from the summit are extraordinary — and the trails are yours almost alone.

Many climbers combine Mount Meru as an acclimatisation ascent before Kilimanjaro — our guides highly recommend this sequencing for a better summit success rate.

Mount Kilimanjaro

JanFeb MarAprMay JunJulAug SepOct NovDec

Two main trekking windows: January–February (warm, some clouds) and June–October (dry season, clearest summit views). Avoid the long rains of March–May.

Mount Meru

JanFeb MarAprMay JunJulAug SepOct NovDec

Similar seasons to Kilimanjaro with a slightly larger January–February window. June–October provides the most stable conditions and clearest views of Kilimanjaro from the summit.

Mount Kenya

JanFeb MarAprMay JunJulAug SepOct NovDec

January–February is actually a superb window on Mount Kenya — drier than August on some routes. The long dry season July–September gives the most reliable summit conditions.
